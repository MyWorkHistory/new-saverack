2. Definitions

For the purposes of this Agreement, the following terms shall have the meanings set forth below: "ASN" (Advanced Shipment Notice) means the submission created by the Client in the WMS prior to inventory arriving at Save Rack, detailing the contents and expected delivery of inbound shipments. "Ready to Ship" means an order status indicating that an order is fully ready for fulfillment, including valid shipping details, available inventory, no account or system holds, and no issues preventing shipment. "On-Hold Order" means any order that cannot be fulfilled due to issues including, but not limited to, missing or incorrect information, insufficient inventory, account status, or manual holds. "Inventory" means all goods, products, or materials owned by the Client and stored, handled, or processed by Save Rack. "Order" means any request submitted through the WMS or integrated systems for the picking, packing, and shipment of Inventory. "SKU" (Stock Keeping Unit) means a unique identifier assigned to each product or product variation for tracking and fulfillment purposes. "WMS" (Warehouse Management System) means the software platform used by Save Rack to manage inventory, orders, shipments, and account activity. "Fulfillment Services" means the services provided by Save Rack, including but not limited to receiving, storage, picking, packing, shipping, returns processing, and related logistics services. "Custom Work" means any service requested by the Client that falls outside of standard Fulfillment Services and may be subject to additional charges. "Backorder" means any Order or product quantity that cannot be fulfilled at the time of processing due to insufficient available Inventory and will remain pending until Inventory becomes available or further instructions are provided by the Client. "Allocation" means the process of reserving specific Inventory quantities for pending Orders, sales channels, or operational purposes within the WMS. "Receiving" means the process of accepting, unloading, inspecting, counting, and processing inbound Inventory delivered to Save Rack, including verification against the ASN and placement into storage locations.
